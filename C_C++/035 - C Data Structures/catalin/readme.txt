telos programmatos

PROSOXI MIN DOSETE ARITHMOUS MONO STRINGS!!!

Arxika to programma perni ws parametro tis frasis N pou tha trexi
stin sinexia auto desmevi mnimi se enan pointer typou stack kai se enan pointer typou queue
grafoume tis metavlites pou tha xrisimopoisume sto programma
To N perni tin timi tis parametrou
epomenos mas zita tin frash pou theloume na exetasoume an einai palindromos
gia na tin pari xrisimopoia tin sinartisi gets() apo tin vivliothiki string.h h opoia voitha na paroume to string xwris spaces
dimiourgi epomenos enan adio stack me voithia twn sinartiseon pou egrapsame stin vivliothiki library_queue-stack.h
dino se mia kenuria metavliti newsize tin timi size tou string tin opoia vrisko me to strlen episis apo tin string.h

h newsize voitha na paroume to size ton xaraktiron apo to a-z kai A-Z

to programma epomenos apothikeui me tin voithia tis push kai enqueue pou exume grapsi tous xaraktires mesa sto stack kai queue analoga

kai an parousiasti kapoio special character meionete to newsize na exume tin pragmatiki timi ton xaraktironmas kai theseon sto stack kai queue

epomenos me enan elegxo apo j=0 mexri newsize
kano olous tou xaraktires mou lowercase gia na mboro na tous sigkrino me tin sinartisis tolower() apo tin vivliothiki ctype.h 
An se auto ton elegxo paratirisoume diafora xaraktiron exume tis diometavlites c1 kai c2 h opoies dinoun timi se mia metavliti trigger h opoia analoga me tin timi tis mas typoni an einai palindromos h oxi.
TELOS apodesmvo mnimi apo ta stack kai queue analoga gia na min yparxoun memory leaks.
